Smart Lights

How do I control smart lights?

You can control smart lights using a smartphone app, voice assistants like Alexa or Google Assistant, or a remote control.

Are smart lights energy-efficient?

Yes, smart lights use LED technology, which is highly energy-efficient and consumes less power than traditional bulbs.
